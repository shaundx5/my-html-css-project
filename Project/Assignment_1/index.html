About Us

We’re a neighborhood restaurant focused on fresh, seasonal ingredients and warm hospitality. Our menu changes with the market, and everything is cooked to order.

Open for lunch and dinner, seven days a week.

Our Chefs

From the culinary capitals of France and Italy, our head chefs came to India to share their craft — and to spread a little love through every plate. Expect classic techniques, seasonal produce, and soul-warming flavors.

The Spice&Spoon Alchemists

Our diverse Franco-Italian team brings a wealth of culinary expertise and creativity to every dish in Bangalore. We hope you enjoy your food just as much as we enjoyed making it.
